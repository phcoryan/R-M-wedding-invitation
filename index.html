Our Love Story

A few moments that shaped our journey.

June 2018
First Meeting

How We Met

Once upon a summer, two strangers shared a workplace, though their paths had not yet truly crossed. She remembered him only by the shade of his shirt—a vibrant purple, her favorite color—never dreaming that the man wearing it would one day hold her heart. The summer faded, and two months passed in silence, until a single message changed everything. He reached out, sending her a group photograph from those sun-drenched days, offering it as a 'copy and a remembrance.' What began as a simple memory quickly sparked a conversation that refused to end. A few weeks later, they shared their first date, realizing that the 'remembrance' wasn't just a look back at the past, but the beautiful beginning of their forever.

December 2019
First Date

The Sweetest First Date

After two months of waiting, the day finally arrived for their very first date. They met at SM Naga, where the busy crowds seemed to disappear the moment they saw each other. There, they shared ice cream, and with every sweet spoonful, the conversation grew easier and their smiles grew brighter. What started as a 'remembrance' photo from a summer job had led them to this simple, perfect moment. As they walked through the mall, ice cream in hand, they realized that the girl who loved purple and the boy who wore it were no longer strangers. In the middle of a simple afternoon, they found a sweetness that would last a lifetime.

November 2023
Proposal

A Promise in the Province

The seeds of their forever were planted early in 2025. While she thought it was just a simple trip home, he was quietly booking flights and preparing for the most important journey of his life. He wasn't just planning a visit; he was planning a promise. In August, the magic truly began. His family traveled from Camarines Sur to Sorsogon for the Pamamanhikan, bringing two families together under one roof. Surrounded by the people who raised them and the warmth of their shared faith, the air was filled with a special kind of peace. There, in front of everyone they loved, he revealed the secret he had been carrying. With his family by his side and hers looking on, he gave her a bracelet—a beautiful symbol of his devotion and his desire to marry her. With a heart full of sincerity, he pledged his love. In the quiet beauty of Sorsogon, a summer 'remembrance' became a sacred commitment, and two families officially became one.
